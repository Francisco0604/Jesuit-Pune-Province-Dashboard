## IV. UX DESIGN FOR SENIOR USERS
1. Large, high-contrast typography (EB Garamond & Inter).
2. Generous padding and large click targets for all buttons.
3. Sticky header ensures navigation is never lost during scrolling.
4. Clear, descriptive action labels (e.g., "Start Exploring Now", "Browse All News").
